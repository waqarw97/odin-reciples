Smoothie recipe

This yogurt smoothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.

This smoothie is delicious. If you are looking for a sweet treat that is healthy, look no further. This smoothie contain fresh fruits, so it is a great source of vitamins. Fruits are naturally sweet, so it is also a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon of milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. That's it!
